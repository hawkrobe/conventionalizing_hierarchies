Instructions

Fill the orders!

In this experiment you will play a fun communication game with another turker! Imagine that you work together in a warehouse for the Shape Company and your goal is to fill orders for customers. The company only sells these eight objects:

There are two jobs, which you and your partner will take turns doing. The Speaker sees the target set (i.e. which object or objects need to be packed up) and must communicate this information to the Listener who must pack them.

For each order, we will pick four of these eight objects and show them to both of you. The objects will be shown in different positions on your different screens. The objects in the target set will be outlined in black for the Speaker. Only the Speaker can see which objects are in the order. In the example below, there is only one object in the target set (the striped circle in the upper-left corner), but other orders may have two objects in the target set!

The goal of the Speaker is to send a message so that the Listener can correctly fill the order. If you are the Listener, you will first need to wait until the Speaker has sent their message before being able to click on the objects you think they're telling you about. You'll both get a bonus of $0.05 when you correctly fill the order (i.e. when the Listener selects exactly the objects in the target set), so pay attention!

What makes this game more interesting and challenging is the content of your messages: instead of using the English language, you get to make up a language together! Both you and your partner will see an array of 16 messages that could be part of this language: to send one of the messages, the Speaker can click and drag it into the grey 'dropzone' below.

Once the Listener clicks on the object or objects they believe are in the target set, they must click 'Done' and both players will be given feedback. The Speaker will see what the Listener clicked, and the Listener will see the true target set. Then you will both automatically advance to the next order and switch roles, so the Listener will become the Speaker and vice versa. There are a total of 96 rounds. After the final round you will answer a few more questions and be on your way.

A few final notes: First, since you are playing with another player, you may see this screen before the game begins:

Just hold tight. Another player should join the game within the next few minutes. If you're in the waiting room 15 minutes without another player joining, please return the HIT and try again later!

Second, please be respectful of the worker you're playing with: pay close attention and try to be as responsive as possible.

Finally, please DO NOT refresh the page at any point once you start the game (including the waiting page). If you refresh, you will not be able to rejoin the game you were in and you may not be able to submit the HIT.
Thank you!
